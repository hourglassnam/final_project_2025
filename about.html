About Us

Cosfarm specializes in manufacturing high-quality, easy-to-use fertilizers and topsoil products designed specifically for agricultural professionals. Established in December 2007, we have been supplying diverse fertilizer solutions both domestically and internationally while supporting government initiatives for increased food production and agricultural advancement.

Our ISO-certified production facility utilizes proprietary technology to produce premium agricultural materials that optimize operational efficiency and crop yields. We work directly with agricultural enterprises to provide customized formulations addressing specific cultivation requirements and environmental conditions.

Core product offerings include our patented compressed plate topsoil system and specialized fertilizer technologies, engineered to enhance resource utilization while reducing application costs for our clients.

Following our manufacturing capacity expansion in 2020, Cosfarm is well-positioned to meet growing market demands through strategic partnerships with key industry stakeholders, ensuring continuous advancement in agricultural technology solutions.
